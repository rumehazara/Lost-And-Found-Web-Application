About Us

Anurag Engineering College Lost & Found is a dedicated platform designed to help the students, staff, and visitors of Anurag Engineering College, Kodad easily report, track, and recover lost items on campus. We understand how stressful it can be to lose personal belongings, and our goal is to make the process quick, simple, and reliable.

<

Our Purpose

Our platform bridges the gap between lost and found items by creating a centralized system where the entire college community can participate. Whether it’s a wallet, laptop, ID card, or any personal item, our mission is to ensure that lost items are returned to their rightful owners with minimal hassle.

Our Vision

To create a safe, organized, and efficient system for managing lost and found items within Anurag Engineering College, fostering a supportive and responsible community.

Our Mission

Provide an easy-to-use interface for reporting and searching lost and found items. Connect finders and owners quickly to minimize stress and inconvenience. Maintain accurate records and a secure database for all reported items. Encourage honesty, responsibility, and community cooperation on campus.

Why Choose Our Platform?

User-Friendly: Simple and intuitive interface for posting and searching items. Fast Communication: Get notifications and connect directly with finders or owners. Secure & Reliable: Only verified members of the college community can access and post items. Community-Oriented: Strengthen trust and cooperation among students and staff.

< At Anurag Engineering College Lost & Found, we believe no lost item should stay lost forever. Our platform is here to make sure that every item finds its way home safely.
